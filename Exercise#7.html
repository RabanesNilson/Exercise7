The main purpose of this tree planting event is multifaceted. Firstly, it serves as an educational platform, allowing participants to understand the ecological significance of trees and their role in maintaining a balanced environment. Secondly, by engaging students, faculty, and the broader community in this endeavor, we seek to foster a spirit of unity and shared responsibility towards our environment. Lastly, we acknowledge the tangible benefits of planting trees, such as improved air quality, carbon sequestration, and enhanced biodiversity, all of which contribute to a healthier and more sustainable future for our region.
